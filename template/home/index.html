About Us



MECON Electricals is a name that stands for quality and reliability. It was established in Sharjah and was previously known as Spencer’s Electricals. In 2007, the company relocated its headquarters to Ajman New Industrial Area and has since carried out its operations from there.

In today’s competitive market producing quality product at the right price has made MECON Electricals a leading electrical switchgear assembler in the Middle East. Our company is approved with all major Electrical Authorities in the U.A.E. such as ADWEA, DEWA, FEWA and SEWA. Our panels are tested by ASTA up to 2500A.

Through rigorous testing our solutions guarantee reliability of the highest standard. Over the years our company has expanded considerably with the development of product range and best technical standard, gaining trust of our esteemed clients and partners.
